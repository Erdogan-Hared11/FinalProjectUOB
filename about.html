Our Company

Dalag Company is a modern enterprise dedicated to directly connecting Somali farmers with consumers, eliminating unnecessary middlemen. Its mission is to ensure farmers get fair market access while consumers receive high-quality produce straight from the source.

The company uses technology to simplify communication, marketing, and distribution of agricultural products. It also offers technical support, training, and market guidance to boost productivity and income.
